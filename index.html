Guesthouse is part coworking space, part bed and breakfast, occasional adventure guide, and 100% yours.

An exclusive, one-of-a-kind stay.

Every team is different, and so is every Guesthouse. Our reimagined hospitality experience is designed around you and your team's needs — complete with a house captain, a private chef, and a local guide and driver for those times when adventure calls.

Fine-tuned for presence.

From the communal spaces to your private quarters; the chef-prepared meals and the inspiring objects found around the home; everything is designed to allow you and your team to be inspired and in the moment, focusing on the big ideas that will drive you and your business forward.

All-inclusive and all you.

The moment you book a stay with us, we begin preparing everything you might need during your stay. From the number of whiteboards to your favorite evening cocktail, everything is tailored to you and it's all included.
